Computers are too easy

Computers are too easy to use. I think Steve Jobs and Bill Gates did disservice to the computer industry. Some people are saying I’m wrong and that it’s Apple and Microsoft that made computers what they are today. I say they are wrong. Apple and Microsoft may be putting out these innovative solutions but they are not creating them.

Microsoft does not make products, it buys them and makes changes to them so they are more user friendly, insecure and inferior to competing products. Microsoft’s secret to success was when Bill Gates sold IBM a piece of software he didn’t even own. He used their money to buy CP/M and made MS-DOS.

The computer industry of today was partially Microsoft. The biggest impact on the computer industry is Gaming. Games push the hardware and games push the software. A computer built in 2007 can still be used for word processing, spreadsheets, web development and browsing. You don’t need a 2GB graphics card to browse the web.
